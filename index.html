Privacy Policy for TempContacts

Last updated: August 4, 2025

Thank you for using TempContacts ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information. Our core principle is privacy: your data is your own, and our app is designed to keep it that way.

Information We Access

To provide its full functionality, TempContacts requires access to certain information and permissions on your device. All of this information is processed locally on your device only and is never uploaded, collected, or shared with us or any third party.

The permissions we request and why we need them:

  • Contacts (`READ_CONTACTS`, `WRITE_CONTACTS`): Required to read your address book to display contact names, and to save, edit, or delete contacts as you direct.
  • Call Log (`READ_CALL_LOG`): Required to display your call history in the "Call Logs" tab.
  • Phone (`CALL_PHONE`, `READ_PHONE_STATE`, `READ_PHONE_NUMBERS`, `MANAGE_OWN_CALLS`): These are required to initiate phone calls, handle dual-SIM selection, and to power the optional "Callback Reminder" feature by detecting when a call has ended.
  • Accounts (`GET_ACCOUNTS`): Required to show you a list of available accounts (e.g., Google, SIM) where you can choose to save a permanent contact.
  • Notifications & Services (`POST_NOTIFICATIONS`, `FOREGROUND_SERVICE`, `FOREGROUND_SERVICE_PHONE_CALL`): Required for the optional "Callback Reminder" feature to run in the background and show you notifications.
  • Vibration (`VIBRATE`): Required to provide haptic feedback on the dialer keypad.
  • Billing (`com.android.vending.BILLING`): Required to handle the optional one-time purchase to upgrade to the Pro version of the app.

Data Storage and Security

All data accessed by TempContacts, including your contacts, call logs, and any temporary contact or reminder information you create, is stored in a secure, private database on your device only. This information is not accessible to other apps and is deleted if you uninstall TempContacts.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
